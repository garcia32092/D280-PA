Phoenix, Arizona

Phoenix is the capital and largest city in Arizona, known for its warm weather and vibrant culture...

  • Population: According to the 2020 Census, Phoenix had a population of approximately 1.7 million people, making it the most populous city in Arizona and the fifth-most populous city in the United States.
  • Year Incorporated: Phoenix was incorporated as a city in 1881.
  • Region: Located in the south-central part of the state.
  • Classification: Urban.
  • Average Income: The median household income in Phoenix was about $60,914, according to the U.S. Census Bureau's 2019 estimates, which is slightly below the median for the entire state of Arizona at that time.

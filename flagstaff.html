Flagstaff, Arizona

  • Population: According to the 2020 Census, Flagstaff had a population of approximately 75,000 people.
  • Year Incorporated: Flagstaff was incorporated in 1894.
  • Region: Flagstaff is located in northern Arizona and is the largest city in Northern Arizona.
  • Classification: Urban/Suburban. Flagstaff is home to Northern Arizona University, contributing to its urban character, but it also maintains a suburban feel with its surrounding forests.
  • Average Income: The median household income in Flagstaff was around $58,000 according to recent estimates, which is somewhat higher than some other parts of the state but remains lower than Phoenix.
